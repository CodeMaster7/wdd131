Photosnap Site Plan

Site Name

Photosnap

This name represents a modern, dynamic photography platform that allows photographers to share and monetize their work. The name combines "photo"with "snap,"suggesting both professional photography and quick, instant moments.

Site Purpose

Photosnap is a platform designed for photographers to share their stories and work. The site provides:

  • A marketplace for photographers to showcase and sell their work
  • Tools and features for creating photo stories
  • Professional photo hosting and sharing capabilities
  • Subscription plans for both creators and consumers

Scenarios

  • How can I showcase my photography portfolio and reach potential clients?
  • What pricing plans are available for professional photographers?
  • How can I create and share photo stories with my audience?
  • What features are included in the different subscription tiers?
